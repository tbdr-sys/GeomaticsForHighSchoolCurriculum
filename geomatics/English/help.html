Note: These instructions only apply for users of Windows 95/98.
To get a resolution of 800 x 600:
  1. On a clean desktop, right click on your desktop.
  2. Click on Properties in the drop down menu.
  3. Go to Settings and move the resolution bar to 800 x 600 pixels.
  4. Click OK.

NOTE: In older computers, there may be a possibility that you cannot adjust it to 800 x 600 pixels. In that case, change it to the highest resolution that your computer allows.
