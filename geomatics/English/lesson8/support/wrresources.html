Space for Species

http://www.spaceforspecies.ca/

Space for Species is a unique educational program that enables students to monitor migratory species and their habitats from beyond the Earth's atmosphere. Developed by the Canadian Space Agency, the Canadian Wildlife Federation, The Canada Centre for Remote Sensing, and the Canadian Wildlife Service, with assistance from corporate sponsors, Space for Species is a web-based learning experience that brings science and technology to life in your classroom.
