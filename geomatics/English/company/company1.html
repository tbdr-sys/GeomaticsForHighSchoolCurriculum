GeoConnections: is a national partnership initiative led by Natural Resources Canada. Through GeoConnections, governments are working with industry and academia to build the Canadian Geospatial Data Infrastructure (CGDI), which will make Canada's geographic ("geospatial") databases, tools and services readily accessible via the Internet. The goal of GeoConnections is to make Canadian geospatial information, tools and services accessible on the Internet for all Canadians through partnerships. GeoConnections has been a major contributor to this project by providing funding and technical guidance.
