Woodroffe High School and OCDSB: have been successful in establishing Geomatics as part of their expected curriculum. The Board has fully supported this project and will be hosting and maintaining the Geomatics web site for use by educators throughout the country. The geography students and staff at Woodroffe High School have had the opportunity to test/develop the lessons to meet the unique needs of educators struggling to offer Geomatics as part of their courses. The students have been invaluable in web site design, lesson development, and testing the overall package as an educational tool.
