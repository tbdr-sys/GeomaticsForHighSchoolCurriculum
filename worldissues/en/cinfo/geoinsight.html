GeoInsight Corp. : has been developing geomatics applications since 1973. GeoInsight Corp. developed GIS two lessons, which explore several aspects of world sustainable agriculture and the global economy. The lessons lead students through many common GIS and mapping functions as they learn about the lesson topics. GeoInsight Corp. has provided data, lesson development, and background information to the development of the lesson packages.
